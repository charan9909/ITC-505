The Impact of Exercise on Mental Health

Authors: Jane Doe, John Smith

Abstract

This paper examines the relationship between physical exercise and mental health, highlighting the benefits of regular physical activity on psychological well-being.

Introduction

Physical health and mental health are closely intertwined. Numerous studies have shown that regular exercise can alleviate symptoms of anxiety and depression.

Methods

This study involved a review of literature from 2000 to 2023, focusing on peer-reviewed articles discussing the psychological effects of exercise.

Results

The findings indicate that participants who engaged in regular exercise reported lower levels of stress and improved mood.

Conclusion

Incorporating exercise into daily routines can significantly enhance mental health, suggesting that healthcare providers should encourage physical activity as a therapeutic measure.
